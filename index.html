ABOUT

~ at the intersection of data and art ~

Hi! I'm Nikki, a data analyst based in Washington, DC. I have a strong background in mathematics and data analysis, proficiency in Python, SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, and a passion for extracting insights from complex datasets. I have experience with the full data lifecycle, from accurately capturing data, through data preparation, analysis, and management, all the way to impactful and concise presentation of insights. I am a self-taught coder and have been developing my data analytics skills since September 2020. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics from UNC Chapel Hill in 2020.
